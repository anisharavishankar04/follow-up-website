Why Follow-Up Appointments Are Essential

Regular follow-ups allow healthcare providers to monitor your health and catch any issues early.

Ensuring medications are working effectively and adjusting dosages as needed.

Better management of chronic conditions like diabetes, hypertension, etc.

Early detection of potential health problems can prevent serious issues down the road.


Risks of Missing Your Follow-Up Appointments

Missing appointments can delay the diagnosis of serious health conditions.

Health issues might worsen without regular monitoring.

Ignoring follow-ups can lead to more serious health problems, which might require more intensive and expensive treatments.
